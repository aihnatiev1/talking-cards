Talking Cards is the best app for developing your child's speech from ages 1 to 5. 234 colorful interactive flashcards with sounds and sentences in Ukrainian.

HOW IT WORKS
Your child sees a bright card, taps it — and hears the sound and sentence. Swipe for the next card. Simple, clear, and fun! Perfect for daily practice at home or on the go.

8 THEMED PACKS
- Speech Sounds — onomatopoeia and first syllables
- Animals — domestic and wild animals with sounds
- At Home — family, daily routines, household items
- Emotions — naming feelings for emotional intelligence
- Transport — cars, trains, planes with sounds
- Food & Fruits — healthy eating in pictures
- Colors & Shapes — learn colors, shapes, and sizes
- Body Parts — getting to know your body
