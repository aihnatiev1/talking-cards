Talking Cards is a colorful, interactive flashcard app with sounds and sentences designed to develop speech skills in toddlers aged 1-5.

HOW IT WORKS
Your child sees a bright card, taps it — and hears the sound and sentence. Swipe for the next card. Simple, clear, and fun!

WHAT'S INSIDE
- 6 themed packs: speech, animals, transport, household, food, emotions
- 174 cards with professional Ukrainian voiceover
- Each card: onomatopoeia + sentence + vivid illustration

FEATURES
- Card of the Day — a new card every day for practice
- Guess the Sound — a quiz to reinforce learning
- Favorites — save the most interesting cards
- Statistics — track your child's progress
- Daily streaks — motivate your child to practice every day

PERFECT FOR
- Speech development and vocabulary building
- Exploring the world around us
- At-home speech therapy practice
- Quality time with your child

SAFE FOR KIDS
- No ads
- Parental controls for settings
- Works offline — no internet needed
